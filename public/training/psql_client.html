PostgreSQL

Using psql client


In windows

You have to add C:\Program Files\PostgreSQL\8.0\bin; to your path.
Open command prompt.
Type,

psql -Upostgres database_name

Now you are ready to operate your database.

type,

\h for helps,
\h CREATE for help on create,
\? for list of commands,
\q to exit
